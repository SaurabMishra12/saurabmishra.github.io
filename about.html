Hello World!

I'm Saurab Mishra, a passionate AI Research Engineer with deep expertise in machine learning, large language models, and distributed intelligence systems. My journey has been driven by the vision to create AI solutions that are both innovative and ethically aligned.

What I Do

I specialize in developing intelligent systems that can learn, adapt, and make autonomous decisions. My research focuses on cutting-edge AI technologies:

Machine Learning & Deep Learning

Advanced neural networks, deep architectures, and intelligent optimization

Large Language Models (LLMs)

Transformer architectures, fine-tuning, and conversational AI systems

Multi-Agent Systems (MAS)

Collaborative AI, agent coordination, and distributed problem-solving

Retrieval-Augmented Generation

Knowledge integration, contextual AI, and hybrid generative systems

RLHF & Ethical AI

Human feedback integration, AI alignment, and responsible development

Distributed AI Systems

Scalable architectures, cloud deployment, and edge computing

Research Philosophy

I believe in developing AI systems that are not only technically advanced but also ethically responsible and aligned with human values. My research combines theoretical innovation with practical applications to create solutions that benefit society.
